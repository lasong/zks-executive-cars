## Information we collect

When you make a booking through our website, we collect:

- **Name** — so our driver knows who they are collecting
- **Email address** — to send you booking-related correspondence
- **Phone number** — to send you an SMS booking confirmation and so our driver can contact you
- **Pickup and drop-off locations** — to plan and fulfil your journey
- **Vehicle class selected** — to allocate the correct vehicle
- **Booking date and time** — recorded automatically when you submit your booking

We do not collect payment information through this website.

## How we use your information

We use the information you provide to:

- Create and manage your booking
- Send you an SMS confirmation with your booking reference and journey details
- Allow our driver to contact you ahead of your journey
- Maintain records of bookings for customer service and accounting purposes

The legal basis for this processing is **performance of a contract** — we need this information to provide the car hire service you have requested.

## Location search

When you start typing a pickup or drop-off location, we send your search text to a third-party address lookup service (a free, OpenStreetMap-based geocoding service) to suggest matching addresses. This happens as you type, before you submit a booking, and is used only to help you select an accurate location.

## SMS messages

We use a third-party SMS provider to deliver your booking confirmation message. Your phone number and the details of your booking (reference, route, vehicle class and booking time) are shared with this provider solely for the purpose of sending that message.

## How long we keep your information

We retain booking records for as long as necessary to fulfil the booking and to meet our legal, accounting and customer service obligations, after which they are deleted.

## Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share it with:

- The driver assigned to your journey
- The third-party services described above (location search and SMS delivery), strictly for the purposes stated

## Your rights

Under UK data protection law, you have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Ask us to correct inaccurate data
- Ask us to delete your data
- Object to or restrict how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details on our [Contact](/contact) page. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the [Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)](https://ico.org.uk).
